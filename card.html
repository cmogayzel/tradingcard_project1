Bald Eagle

Bald eagles are not actually bald but the white feathers on their head, their name is based on the old definition of "bald".

  • Scientific Name:Haliaeetus leucocephalus
  • Average Wing span: 90 cm
  • Average Life Span: 20 years
  • Habitat: Nest in forested areas near large bodies of water.
